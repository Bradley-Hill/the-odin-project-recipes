Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 125g Butter
  • 250g Brown Sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 375g self-raising flour
  • 150g chocolate chips

Method

  1. Step 1 Melt the butter and combine with the sugar
  2. Step 2 Beat the egg and add to the sugar/butter mix
  3. Step 3 Mix in the chocolate chips whilst slowly adding the flour until all combined
  4. Step 4 Bake in an oven at 180 degrees Centigrade for 10-15 minutes
  5. Step 5 Enjoy!
