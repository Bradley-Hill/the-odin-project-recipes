Sweet Potato Curry

Ingredients

  • 2/3 sweet potatos
  • 300g spinach
  • 2 tins of chickpeas
  • 1 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin of coconut milk
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of fresh ginger
  • 2 medium onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Method

  1. Step 1 Chop the onions and peel and slice the garlic cloves. Peel the ginger ready to be grated, and drain the tins of chickpeas and rinse them.Chop the sweet potatos into rough chunks.Rnse the spinach.
  2. Step 2 Sweat the onions and add the chopped garlic after 5 minutes.Grate the fresh ginger and add to the mix.
  3. Step 3 Add the sweet potatos and fry for 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4 Add the tinned tomatoes and coconut milk, bring to a simmer.
  5. Step 5 Add the chickpeas and cook on low-medium heat for 20 minutes.
  6. Step 6 Stir in the spinach and turn of the heat. Ready to serve.
